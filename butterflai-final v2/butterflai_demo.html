↯ How It Works

Six stages. Zero friction.

Every step is automated. You only interact where your judgment matters.

1
Describe in one sentence
Type your ML goal in plain English. ButterflAI parses intent, infers modality, task type, and expected output format automatically.
Natural language → structured plan
2
AI finds the best dataset
Searches Kaggle (25M+ datasets) and HuggingFace Hub, ranks by semantic relevance. Results cached 7 days in Supabase — no redundant API calls.
Kaggle + HuggingFace · 7-day cache
3
Configure with AI-prefilled hyperparams
Every hyperparameter is pre-filled. Choose between Gemini Flash (free), Groq Llama 3 (fastest, free), or Claude Haiku (best code). All auto-fallback.
Gemini · Groq · Claude
4
AI validates and auto-fixes inconsistencies
10 consistency checks across dataset, config, and code. OOM risk? Wrong loss function? Class mismatch? The AI rewrites the broken section and shows you a line-level diff with the reason for every change.
Auto-fix + diff + reason = full transparency
5
Live training on real GPUs
Your job runs on Modal.com T4 GPUs. Logs stream in real time via SSE. Accuracy and loss curves update live, epoch by epoch. All metrics stored in Supabase.
Modal T4 16GB · SSE live stream
6
Download model + launch Streamlit demo
Download best_model.pth, run the generated streamlit_app.py, or get a shareable URL. All outputs synced to Google Drive. History forever in Supabase.
Drive sync · Supabase history · Streamlit deploy
